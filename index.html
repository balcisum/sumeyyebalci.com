Welcome. I offer psychological counseling in a safe, respectful, and supportive space. In my practice I am using Acceptance Committment Therapy, aspects of Schema Therapy and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. I have worked with clients from different life stages, including young adults, older adults, and different problem areas, such as dealing with life changes(new job, school, changes in relationship status), new diagnosis, and various psychological problems.
